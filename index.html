4 KEY LESSONS

James Clear calls these the 4 Laws of Behaviour Change.

Law 1 — Make It Obvious

Put good habits where you can see them. Want to read more? Put the book on your pillow. Out of sight means out of mind.

Law 2 — Make It Attractive

Pair your habit with something you enjoy. Only listen to your favourite podcast while exercising. Make it something to look forward to.

Law 3 — Make It Easy

Start tiny. Do not say I will exercise for 1 hour. Say I will do 2 push-ups. Make it so easy you cannot say no.

Law 4 — Make It Satisfying

Reward yourself after completing a habit. Your brain loves rewards. Use that to your advantage.
